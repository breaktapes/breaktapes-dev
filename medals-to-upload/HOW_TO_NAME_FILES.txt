HOW TO NAME YOUR MEDAL PHOTOS
==============================

RULE: {race-name-slugified}-{year}.png

Slugify = lowercase, spaces to hyphens, no special chars.

EXAMPLES:
  Dubai Marathon 2026       → dubai-marathon-2026.png
  London Marathon 2025      → london-marathon-2025.png
  Ras Al Khaimah HM 2026    → ras-al-khaimah-half-marathon-2026.png
  Boston Marathon 2024      → boston-marathon-2024.png
  Berlin Marathon 2024      → berlin-marathon-2024.png
  Tokyo Marathon 2025       → tokyo-marathon-2025.png
  Chicago Marathon 2024     → chicago-marathon-2024.png
  New York Marathon 2024    → new-york-city-marathon-2024.png
  UTMB 2024                 → utmb-mont-blanc-2024.png
  Comrades Marathon 2024    → comrades-marathon-2024-gold.png
                              comrades-marathon-2024-silver.png
                              comrades-marathon-2024-bill-rowan.png
                              comrades-marathon-2024-bronze.png
                              comrades-marathon-2024-vic-clapham.png
                              comrades-marathon-2024-back.png
  Ironman Triathlon 2024    → ironman-triathlon-2024.png
  Ironman 70.3 Dubai 2024   → ironman-703-dubai-2024.png

TIPS:
  - White background photos work best (background is auto-removed)
  - PNG or JPG both fine
  - Max 5MB per file
  - First photo uploaded for a race becomes the community photo for ALL users
  - You can upload multiple years for the same race (different filenames)

COMRADES VARIANTS (by finish time):
  gold        = under 6:00
  silver      = 6:00 – 7:30
  bill-rowan  = 7:30 – 9:30
  bronze      = 9:30 – 10:00
  vic-clapham = 10:00 – 11:00
  back        = 11:00 – 12:00
